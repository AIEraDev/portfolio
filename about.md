# About Me

Abdulkabir Musa is an AI Platform Engineer and the Co-founder & CEO of AIEraDev, dedicated to advancing AI education and development in Africa. With a Computer Science degree from the University of Ibadan, he combines deep technical expertise in Machine Learning and Full Stack development to build intelligent, context-aware systems.

Abdulkabir is a "context-first" product thinker who believes in software that proactively solves problems. His technical arsenal includes Python, TypeScript, Next.js, and the Model Context Protocol (MCP). He has architected diverse projects, from *Phantom*, a real-time coding battle platform, to scalable e-commerce and transit solutions.

Beyond code, he is a thought leader publishing on software reliability and AI-driven workflows. Abdulkabir is confident in the power of AI to transform industries and is committed to building the infrastructure that will support the next era of intelligent applications.
